About Yamaki

Yamaki Foods brings the authentic taste of health to your table with Shirataki rice and noodles — a traditional Japanese superfood loved for its low calories and high fiber content. Made from the konjac plant, our products are perfect for modern diets including keto, diabetic-friendly, and gluten-free lifestyles.

We are inspired by the simplicity and elegance of Japanese cuisine and culture. Our mission is to bring healthy, clean-label products into every Indian kitchen, offering both taste and nutrition without compromise.

Why Choose Yamaki

Yamaki products are 100% plant-based, contain zero sugar, and support a keto and diabetic-friendly lifestyle.
